European Skull Mount

European Skull Mount

Dylan Snyder

What You'll Need · Instructions

Here is a easy way to create a do-it-yourself european skull mount.

What You'll Need

Tools Ingredients
High-pressure Burner Box of Baking Soda
Propane tank Volume 40 Cream
Large pot High strength soap
Detailing brush Basic White
Rubber gloves
Knife
Garden hose
Bubble wrap
Masking tape
Electric heater
Instructions
  1. Start by skinning the head and removing the lower jaw. This is best done in the field.

  2. Add a large amount of soap to water and bring to a simmer

  3. Put skull into the pot so that the base of the antlers are just above the water. let sit for one hour.


  4. Remove skull from water and cut away any remaining tissue.


  5. Bring a new pot of water to a simmer, and add baking soda.

  6. Place skull back into the pot and let sit for another hour.

  7. Take skull out of the water. Spray with high pressure attachment to wash away loose tissue.

  8. Soak skull in clean pot of warm soapy water for 10 minutes

  9. Let skull dry in front of electric heater until completly dry.

  10. Mix Volume 40 and Basic White together until it thickens

  11. Apply mixture liberally to skull with brush. Make sure to get every little piece of skull, and don't get any on the antlers.


  12. Let sit for an hour and a half.

  13. Wash mixture off skull, and dry with heater. You are then finished
